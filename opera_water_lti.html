Opera Water

Overview

Opera Water is an intelligent, IoT-powered platform designed by LTIMindtree to enhance the operational efficiency of water utilities. It provides real-time monitoring and management of water infrastructure, facilitating better decision-making and resource optimization. The platform integrates data from various sources across the supply and distribution networks, offering comprehensive insights into water usage, quality, and asset performance.

Key Components and Benefits

  1. Real-Time Monitoring:

    2. Description: Opera Water offers real-time visibility into the operational status of water treatment and distribution systems. This feature allows utilities to track water flow, quality, and pressure, helping to quickly identify and address issues such as leaks or equipment failures.

  2. Predictive Maintenance:

    3. Description: The platform uses predictive analytics to forecast potential issues before they occur. This capability helps in scheduling maintenance activities proactively, reducing unplanned downtime and extending the life of assets.

  3. Energy and Cost Optimization:

    4. Description: By analyzing energy and chemical consumption, Opera Water helps utilities optimize these inputs, leading to significant cost savings. The platform also assists in reducing non-revenue water (NRW), which includes water lost before it reaches customers.

  4. Regulatory Compliance:

    5. Description: Opera Water ensures compliance with regulatory standards by monitoring water quality parameters and automating reporting processes.

Specific Backend Tools

AWS IoT Core

Description: AWS IoT Core is utilized for device management and bi-directional communication between devices and the cloud. It supports various protocols like MQTT, HTTPS, and LoRaWAN, enabling secure and efficient data exchange.

AWS IoT Greengrass

Description: This service extends cloud capabilities to local devices, allowing for edge computing. It supports local execution of AWS Lambda functions, facilitating real-time data processing and analysis.

AWS IoT Device Management and Device Defender

Description: These tools help in securely onboarding, monitoring, and managing IoT devices. They ensure that devices operate correctly and securely, providing continuous health and security monitoring.

Amazon Timestream and Amazon Kinesis

Description: Amazon Timestream is used for storing and analyzing time-series data, which is common in IoT applications. Amazon Kinesis handles real-time data streaming, enabling immediate data analysis and response.

Example Scenario

A municipal water utility implemented Opera Water to manage its extensive network of pipes and treatment plants. The platform provided real-time data on water quality and flow rates, enabling the utility to detect leaks and other issues promptly. By using predictive maintenance features, the utility reduced unplanned downtime and optimized the use of chemicals and energy, resulting in significant cost savings and improved service reliability.
