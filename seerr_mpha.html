SEERR Framework

SEERR Framework (Service Experience Enhancement and Repeat Caller Resolution) developed by Mphasis is designed to manage and prioritize service desk interactions, particularly focusing on repeat callers. This framework helps organizations to identify, tag, report, and prioritize users who repeatedly seek updates or resolutions on existing service tickets, ensuring that their issues are addressed efficiently and effectively.

Key Components and Features

  1. Repeat Caller Identification:

    2. Functionality: The SEERR framework automatically tags users who have made multiple requests related to the same issue, ensuring that these repeat interactions are flagged for priority handling.

  2. Issue Prioritization:

    3. Functionality: By prioritizing repeat callers, SEERR ensures that unresolved or recurring issues receive prompt attention, reducing user frustration and improving service efficiency.

  3. Reporting and Analytics:

    4. Functionality: The framework includes tools for generating detailed reports on repeat callers, helping IT teams to identify trends, understand the root causes of recurring issues, and develop strategies to prevent them.

  4. Integration with ITSM Tools:

    5. Functionality: SEERR integrates seamlessly with existing IT Service Management (ITSM) systems, allowing for smooth data flow and consistent tracking of service requests across the organization.

Benefits

  • Enhanced User Experience: By prioritizing repeat callers, SEERR ensures that users receive timely resolutions to their issues, which improves overall satisfaction.
  • Increased Efficiency: The framework reduces the time spent on managing recurring issues by automating the identification and prioritization processes, allowing IT teams to focus on more complex tasks.
  • Proactive Problem Management: With detailed analytics and reporting, SEERR helps organizations to identify and address systemic issues that lead to repeat calls, ultimately reducing the number of recurring incidents.

Backend Tools and Technologies

Automation Engine

Functionality: Powers the tagging and prioritization processes, ensuring that repeat callers are automatically identified and their cases escalated as needed.

Analytics and Reporting Tools

Functionality: Provides insights into service desk performance, highlighting areas where repeat issues occur frequently and enabling proactive measures to address these problems.

ITSM Integration

Functionality: Ensures that SEERR's processes are fully integrated with existing IT service management tools, allowing for seamless tracking and management of service requests.

Simple Example for Better Understanding

Imagine a situation where a user has reported an issue with their email system multiple times over a few days. The SEERR framework would automatically tag this user as a repeat caller, prioritize their ticket, and alert the service desk team to focus on resolving the issue permanently. Additionally, SEERR would generate a report highlighting this repeat issue, enabling the IT team to investigate the underlying cause and prevent similar problems in the future.

Conclusion

The SEERR framework is a vital tool for improving the efficiency and effectiveness of service desks by ensuring that recurring issues are handled promptly and effectively, leading to higher user satisfaction and more efficient IT operations.
