Microland Pillars of Differentiation

Digital Workplace

The Digital Workplace tower encompasses a suite of technologies and services aimed at creating a modern, efficient, and collaborative working environment. This transformation focuses on enhancing user experience, increasing productivity, and enabling seamless collaboration across the enterprise.

Accelerators are specialized tools and solutions designed to expedite and optimize the implementation of these digital workplace initiatives, driving faster and more effective results.

smartWorkspace Digital eXperience Management (DXM) Agnostic Device & Apps Intelligeni Bots M365 Services Unified Endpoint Security Service Desk with AI MicroBots

Hybrid Cloud

The Hybrid Cloud tower is an integrated approach that combines private and public cloud services with on-premises infrastructure. This hybrid model offers greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency, allowing organizations to optimize their IT infrastructure according to their unique needs.

Accelerators in the Hybrid Cloud tower are specialized tools and solutions designed to streamline the deployment, management, and optimization of hybrid cloud environments. These accelerators enable organizations to seamlessly integrate on-premises infrastructure with public and private clouds, ensuring scalability, flexibility, and efficiency in cloud operations.

Mentioned below are their key Accelerators:

Intelligeni Cloud Management Intelligeni Platform Cloud FinOps Cloud Disaster Recovery (Cloud DR) Secure and Compliant Cloud Data Center Transformation Cloud Operations Platform

IoT Tower

The Internet of Things (IoT) tower encompasses technologies and services that connect, manage, and analyze data from physical devices, sensors, and machines. This interconnected ecosystem allows for real-time data collection, monitoring, and automation across various industries, leading to enhanced operational efficiency, improved decision-making, and new business opportunities.

Accelerators in the IoT tower are specialized tools and solutions created to enhance the development, deployment, and management of IoT ecosystems. These accelerators facilitate the rapid connection, monitoring, and analysis of smart devices, enabling organizations to harness the full potential of IoT technologies for improved operational efficiency and innovation.

Mentioned below are their key Accelerators:

IoT Edge Management Digital Twin Technology Augmented Reality (AR) Tools Secure IoT Connectivity

Network

The Network tower focuses on providing robust, secure, and scalable networking solutions that enable seamless connectivity, communication, and data transfer across the entire IT infrastructure. This encompasses various aspects of network design, implementation, management, and optimization.

Security

The Security Tower in IT infrastructure encompasses a comprehensive set of technologies, policies, and practices designed to protect data, applications, systems, and networks from cyber threats and unauthorized access. This tower ensures the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of information while complying with regulatory requirements.

Accelerators in the Security tower are specialized tools and solutions aimed at strengthening an organization's security posture. These accelerators streamline the implementation of security measures, ensuring rapid detection, prevention, and response to threats, thus safeguarding enterprise assets and data in an increasingly complex digital landscape.

Mentioned below are their key Accelerators:

Managed Detection and Response (MDR) Extended Detection and Response (XDR) Security Operations Center as a Service (SOCaaS) Cloud Security Gateways (CSGs) Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) Access Management (IAM) Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA) Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM) Zero Trust Architecture (ZTA) Threat Intelligence Platforms

Sustainability

Accelerators in the Sustainability tower are specialized tools and solutions designed to drive sustainable practices across the enterprise. These accelerators help organizations reduce their environmental impact, optimize resource usage, and achieve sustainability goals by facilitating the integration of eco-friendly technologies and processes into their operations.

Smart Facility Management Green Data Centers Renewable Energy Integration Waste Management Solutions Sustainable Supply Chain Management Water Resource Management
