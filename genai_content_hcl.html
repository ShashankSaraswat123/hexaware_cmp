Simple Example: Personalized Email Campaign

Scenario: A fashion retailer wants to send personalized emails to their customers, promoting new arrivals based on individual preferences.

Steps:

  1. Data Collection: Customer browsing and purchase history data is collected and stored using the Actian Data Platform.
  2. Content Generation: HCL’s ADvantage GenAI Suite’s Content Generator uses this data to create personalized email content for each customer. For example, if a customer frequently browses dresses, the email will highlight new arrivals in the dresses category.
  3. Deployment: The personalized emails are managed and sent out using the HCL Marketing Cloud, ensuring each customer receives a unique email tailored to their preferences.

Backend Tools Used:

  • Actian Data Platform: For integrating and analyzing customer data.
  • HCL’s ADvantage GenAI Suite: Specifically, the Content Generator to create personalized email content.
  • HCL Marketing Cloud: To manage and deploy the email campaign.
