HCL BigFix

HCL BigFix is a comprehensive endpoint management solution designed to secure, manage, and automate IT operations across a wide range of devices and environments. It leverages AI and automation to provide continuous compliance, streamline management processes, and enhance overall security.

What is HCL BigFix?

HCL BigFix is an endpoint management platform that automates the discovery, management, and remediation of all endpoints, whether they are on-premises, virtual, or cloud-based. It supports nearly 100 different operating systems and is designed to ensure continuous compliance and security.

Backend Tools and Technologies

BigFix Platform

Description: The core infrastructure that provides unified endpoint management and security. It includes the BigFix Console, BigFix Server, and BigFix Clients.

BigFix Inventory

Description: A tool for discovering and managing software and hardware assets across the organization, enabling visibility into the IT environment and helping with software license management.

BigFix Patch

Description: A module that automates the process of deploying patches and updates across multiple operating systems, ensuring that all endpoints are up-to-date and secure.

BigFix Compliance

Description: Ensures that all devices comply with security policies and industry regulations, providing continuous monitoring and reporting on compliance status.

BigFix Lifecycle

Description: Provides comprehensive lifecycle management for IT assets, including provisioning, configuration, patching, and retirement.

BigFix Protection

Description: A module that offers advanced security features such as malware protection, vulnerability management, and endpoint protection.

BigFix Remote Control

Description: Allows administrators to remotely access and manage endpoints, providing support and maintenance capabilities.

BigFix Insights

Description: An analytics tool that provides advanced reporting and data visualization capabilities, offering insights into asset utilization, compliance, and security posture.

BigFix Asset Management

Description: Helps in tracking and managing the lifecycle of hardware and software assets, ensuring efficient utilization and compliance.

BigFix Capacity Planning

Description: Tools that help in planning and optimizing the capacity and performance of IT resources.

BigFix Patch Policy Management

Description: Automates the process of patching by defining and enforcing policies for patch deployment across the organization.

BigFix Web Reports

Description: A reporting tool that allows the creation of customized reports and dashboards for real-time insights into the BigFix environment.

BigFix Relay

Description: A component that helps in scaling the BigFix infrastructure by relaying communications between the BigFix Server and clients, reducing the load on the central server.
