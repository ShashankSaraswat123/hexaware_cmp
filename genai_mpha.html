Gen AI Foundry

Gen AI Foundry by Mphasis, developed in collaboration with AWS, is a platform aimed at accelerating the adoption of Generative AI in various industries, particularly financial services. It facilitates the creation of industry-specific use cases and the development of Proof of Concepts (POCs) utilizing advanced AI technologies.

Key Components and Features

  1. Industry-Specific Use Cases:

    2. Description: Focuses on addressing critical challenges like core platform modernization, intelligent document processing, and financial crime investigation.

  2. Collaborative Development:

    3. Description: A dedicated team works closely with clients to develop and prioritize use cases throughout the POC lifecycle.

  3. Advanced AI Tools:

    4. Description: Utilizes AWS products such as Amazon SageMaker, Amazon Bedrock, and advanced language models (LLMs) for rapid AI-driven solution development.

  4. Demonstration and POC Opportunities:

    5. Description: Clients can experience tailored generative AI solutions through demos and POCs at Mphasis and AWS facilities.

Benefits

  • Accelerated AI Adoption: Enables rapid implementation of AI-driven solutions.
  • Operational Efficiency: Automates processes, reducing costs and improving efficiency.
  • Tailored Solutions: Provides industry-specific AI solutions.
  • Innovation and Modernization: Modernizes core systems and leverages cutting-edge AI technologies.

Backend Tools and Their Functionality

Amazon SageMaker

Description: A fully managed service that provides tools to build, train, and deploy machine learning models quickly.

Functionality: Used in Gen AI Foundry to develop and deploy custom machine learning models for industry-specific applications.

Amazon Bedrock

Description: A service that makes it easy to build and scale generative AI applications with foundation models.

Functionality: Powers the creation of generative AI applications, allowing for rapid prototyping and deployment of AI solutions.

Mphasis.ai

Description: The business unit driving AI initiatives within Mphasis, providing expertise and resources for AI adoption.

Functionality: Supports the development, deployment, and management of AI models and solutions through the Gen AI Foundry.

Example for Better Understanding

A large bank wants to modernize its customer service operations by integrating AI-driven chatbots. Using the Gen AI Foundry, the bank can develop a POC with Amazon Bedrock to create a chatbot that uses advanced language models to interact with customers, providing personalized responses and automating routine inquiries. This solution enhances customer engagement and reduces the workload on human agents, leading to improved efficiency and customer satisfaction.
