Hybrid Cloud

The Hybrid Cloud tower is an integrated approach that combines private and public cloud services with on-premises infrastructure. This hybrid model offers greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency, allowing organizations to optimize their IT infrastructure according to their unique needs.

Accelerators in the Hybrid Cloud tower are specialized tools and solutions designed to streamline the deployment, management, and optimization of hybrid cloud environments. These accelerators enable organizations to seamlessly integrate on-premises infrastructure with public and private clouds, ensuring scalability, flexibility, and efficiency in cloud operations.

Mentioned below are their key Accelerators:

Xenon Framework InfraGenie™ Front2Back™ Transformation NextStep IP Accelerators Sparkle Cloud Innovation Lab SAGE (Solution Architect Group) Framework App-Cluster and Release-Plan Frameworks Workstreams Framework

IoT Tower

The Internet of Things (IoT) tower encompasses technologies and services that connect, manage, and analyze data from physical devices, sensors, and machines. This interconnected ecosystem allows for real-time data collection, monitoring, and automation across various industries, leading to enhanced operational efficiency, improved decision-making, and new business opportunities.

Accelerators in the IoT tower are specialized tools and solutions created to enhance the development, deployment, and management of IoT ecosystems. These accelerators facilitate the rapid connection, monitoring, and analysis of smart devices, enabling organizations to harness the full potential of IoT technologies for improved operational efficiency and innovation.

Mentioned below are their key Accelerators:

Embedded AI and IoT Edge Technologies IoT-based Network Management Product Smartification Predictive and Preventive Maintenance IoMT (Internet of Medical Things) Smart Connected Devices (SCD) Edge Computing and AI IoT-based Predictive Analytics
