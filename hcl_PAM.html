Privileged Access Management (PAM) by HCL

Overview

HCL Technologies' Privileged Access Management (PAM) solutions help organizations secure, manage, and monitor privileged accounts to protect critical assets and ensure compliance. PAM solutions address the unique security challenges associated with privileged access by centralizing and streamlining the management of privileged credentials and access controls.

Key Features

  • Centralized Management:
    • User Provisioning: Automates the creation, management, and deactivation of privileged accounts, ensuring only authorized users have access.
    • Access Controls: Implements stringent access controls and policies to limit privileged access based on user roles and responsibilities.
  • Advanced Security Measures:
    • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Enhances security by requiring multiple forms of verification for privileged access.
    • Session Monitoring: Tracks and records all activities performed during privileged sessions, providing full visibility and accountability.
  • Compliance and Governance:
    • Audit and Reporting: Comprehensive reporting tools that ensure compliance with regulatory requirements by providing detailed logs of privileged access and actions.
    • Policy Management: Defines and enforces security policies to ensure consistent and secure management of privileged accounts.
  • Integration and Interoperability:
    • API Integration: Supports seamless integration with various IT systems and applications, enabling centralized control and monitoring.
    • Vendor-Agnostic: Works with leading PAM solutions like CyberArk, BeyondTrust, and others to provide tailored security solutions.

Backend Tools and Technologies

Cloud Platforms

Microsoft Azure and AWS: Provide the scalable and secure infrastructure necessary for managing PAM services.

PAM Platforms

CyberArk and BeyondTrust: Leading PAM solutions that offer comprehensive tools for managing privileged access, including password vaulting, session recording, and risk analysis.

Security Automation and Orchestration

Splunk Phantom and IBM Resilient: Tools for automating security workflows and coordinating incident response activities.

Data Analytics

Elasticsearch and Kibana: Real-time data search, analysis, and visualization tools that provide insights into privileged access activities.

Example Use Case

Scenario: A healthcare organization needs to secure its privileged accounts to protect sensitive patient data and comply with HIPAA regulations. The organization decides to implement HCL’s PAM solution.

Implementation Steps:

  • Assessment and Planning:
    • Conduct a detailed assessment of the current privileged access landscape to identify vulnerabilities and areas for improvement.
    • Develop a comprehensive PAM strategy aligned with HIPAA requirements.
  • Deployment of PAM Solution:
    • Implement CyberArk to manage and secure privileged accounts, ensuring robust password management and session recording.
    • Integrate MFA to enhance the security of privileged access.
  • Continuous Monitoring and Compliance:
    • Use Splunk Phantom for automated incident response and Elasticsearch for real-time monitoring and reporting of privileged activities.

Benefits Realized:

  • Enhanced Security: Improved protection against unauthorized access and data breaches through centralized management and monitoring of privileged accounts.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensured compliance with HIPAA and other regulations through comprehensive audit trails and reporting capabilities.
  • Operational Efficiency: Streamlined management of privileged access, reducing manual workload and enhancing overall security posture.

By adopting HCL’s PAM solution, the healthcare organization achieves a secure, compliant, and efficient framework for managing privileged access, protecting sensitive data, and ensuring regulatory compliance.
